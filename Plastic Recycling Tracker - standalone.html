What to bring

We can only recylce two types of plastic: HDPE(Number 2) and PP(Number 5). Check the recycling number on the bottom of your item before bringing it in. If possible please bring in the plastic with all wrappers removed to save time. Thank you

HDPE

High-density polyethylene
  • Milk jugs and juice bottles
  • Shampoo and detergent bottles
  • Yogurt and butter tubs (some)
  • Bottle caps
  • Hard, opaque plastic containers

PP

Polypropylene
  • Yogurt cups and margarine tubs
  • Takeaway food containers
  • Medicine bottles
  • Straws and bottle caps
  • Reusable food storage
